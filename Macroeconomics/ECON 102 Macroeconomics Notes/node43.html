Adding Government and Trade to Our Model (G and NX)

The government can use taxing and its spending powers to affect the level of national income, called fiscal policy. Additionally, external affairs relating to foreign trade affect the economy.

Here we keep our assumption that prices are fixed, so inflation has no effect.
