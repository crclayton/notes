Changes in Net Exports

When domestic prices rise, domestic goods become more relatively expensive, so the consumers of a country typically purchase more foreign goods, no 68#68. On top of that, foreign purchases see the goods are more expensive relative to other countries, so 69#69. Thus 70#70.
