Increase in Government Purchases

As we now know, an increase in 9#9 will shift 14#14 to the right, increasing real GDP beyond 21#21 in the short run. So factor prices will increase, so wages will increase, so prices will increase, so 15#15 will increase, and finally 2#2 will return to 21#21 but 4#4 levels will have increased.

Let's now consider the effects that in increase in 9#9 may have on 21#21.

Even though 77#77 will return to 21#21, since 9#9 has increased, composition of 21#21 has changed. This means that there must be some fall in 78#78. In which case, we say that government purchases have crowded out private expenditures and potentially capital investment. When private investment is crowded out, this can reduce the future growth rate of 21#21.

However, if government purchases are in public infrastructure that increases private-sector productivity and production, such as highways, bridges, ports, and electrical lines, then 21#21 can also rise and the crowding out effect will be minimized.
