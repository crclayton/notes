Measurement of National Income

National income, GDP, can be calculated three ways.

  1. Add up the value added of all goods and services produced in the economy by each firm. This would give us the value of all domestic output.

  2. Add up the total value of expenditure.

  3. Add up the total value of income.

By definition, all three of these will yield the same result because of the circularity of the economy.
