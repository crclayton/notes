Adjustment process

Recessionary gap, 73#73, surplus of labour, wages fall, prices fall, AS falls, 2#2 increases, gap closes, price level falls, unemployment falls. Takes long because of sticky wages.

Inflationary gap, 23#23, shortages of labour, wages go up, prices go up, AS increases, 2#2 decreases, gap closes, price level increases. Doesn't take long.
