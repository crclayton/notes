Taxation Revenue, 13#13

13#13 do not add directly into 11#11 because they are not an expenditure on goods and services, however taxes indirectly influence 11#11 because they decrease disposable income, 37#37, whereas transfer payments increase 37#37. Net taxes are the difference between tax revenue and transfer payments.

54#54 (16)

Typically taxes are a function of national income so we will treat net taxes, 13#13, as an induced variable. The net tax rate 55#55, is the increase in tax revenue given increase in national income, 56#56. It is not, however, the income-tax rate because it reflects all taxes from all levels of government.
