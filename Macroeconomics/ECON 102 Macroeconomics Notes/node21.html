Factor Payments

The factors of production are Labour, Land, Captial, and Technology/Entrepreneurship. Each of these generate income via, respectively.

  1. Wages - The pre-tax income, including pensions, benefits, etc. that households receive for labour.

  2. Rent - Income households receive for renting land.

  3. Interest - Income households receive for lending money to firms (excludes income from government bonds).

  4. Profits - Income households receive for entrepreneurship. These include distributed profits (dividends) and undistributed profits (retained earnings).
