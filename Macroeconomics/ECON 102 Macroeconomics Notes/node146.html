Terms of Trade

The terms of trade 110#110, determines how the gains from trade are shared - ie. who gets more and who gets less. It relates the quantity of imported goods that can be obtained per unit of goods exported.


111#111 (34)

Essentially, this equation indicates the cost of an exported good. If the 112#112, this is favourable for the country. If the 113#113, this is unfavourable for the country.

Along the TOT curve, it represents the consumption possibilities achievable through trade. When overlayed with the production possibilities curve, it can be shown that consumption can be reached at a point outside the PPC.
