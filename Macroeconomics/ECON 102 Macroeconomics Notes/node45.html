Government Purchases, 9#9

Government purchases, like before, include all government spending excluding transfer payments, plus government investments. All government purchases are included in GDP as goods or services, so 9#9 is a direct component of 11#11.

We will make the assumption that 9#9 is not dependent on the level of national income, rather, it is a function of elections and ideology, so we will treat it as autonomous.
