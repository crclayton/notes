Sources of Economic Growth

  1. Growth in the labour force

  2. Growth in human capital - the quality of the labour force, via education, experience, or training

  3. Growth in physical capital - factories, equipment, communications, etc. We also include improvements in the quality of the capital here.

  4. Technological improvements
