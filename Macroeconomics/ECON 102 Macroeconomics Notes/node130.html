Constant Inflation

Here we are assuming that output is at potential and there aren't any shocks. There are three requirements of constant inflation...

  1. Expectations of inflation - shifts 15#15 up

  2. Expansionary monetary policy by the central bank - shifts 14#14 up

  3. The rate of monetary growth is equal to the expected rate of inflation - 2#2 remains at 21#21

When the central bank increases the money supply to cancel out the anticipated inflation, the bank is said to be validating the exceptions.
