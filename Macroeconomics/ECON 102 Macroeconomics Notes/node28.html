Simple Short-Run Model (C and I)

In this short-run model, we assume that national income (GDP or 2#2) is constant. We also assume that the price level is constant, so there is zero inflation. We will also, for simplicity, ignore the effects of 9#9 (taxes) and 10#10 (trade).
