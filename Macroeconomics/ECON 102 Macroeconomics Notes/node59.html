Slope of AD

It is negatively slope for two reasons.

  1. A fall in price level leads to a rise in private sector wealth, increasing consumption, increasing equilibrium GDP.

  2. A fall in price level leads to a rise in exports and a decrease in imports, increasing equilibrium GDP.
